THE HISTORY BEHIND YOSHI'S ISLAND

THE IDEA:

Yoshi's Island was developed after the idea of the director, John Doe, who wanted to create a game that would be a prequel to the original Super Mario Bros. He wanted to explore the origins of Yoshi and how he came to be Mario's companion. The game was developed by Nintendo and released in 1995 for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System (SNES).

The reception was overwhelmingly positive, with critics praising the game's innovative gameplay, charming graphics, and memorable soundtrack. Yoshi's Island has since become a beloved classic in the Mario franchise and is often cited as one of the greatest video games of all time.
